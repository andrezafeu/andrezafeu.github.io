Maternal Mortality

An analysis of maternal mortality in the USA

Goal of the project:

Understand why the Maternal Mortality for Black women in the USA is much higher than for other races.

Technologies used:

  • Language: Python
  • Libraries: Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn, Numpy, Sklearn, Statsmodels

Skills:

  • Technical / analytical skills: Data cleaning & wrangling, feature engineering, exploratory data analysis, logistic regression, machine learning
  • Soft skills: Leadership, communicating technical concepts to a non technical audience

Development of the project and challenges:

This was the first data science project that I worked on, and I had the opportunity to lead a team of fellows during the Data Science for All Fellowship. The project challenged me to use my recently acquired knowledge to do an analysis of a complex subject in a limited time, as well as to communicate technical concepts to a non technical audience. We also had to quickly pivot to a different analysis after losing 3 team members in the middle of the fellowship.
