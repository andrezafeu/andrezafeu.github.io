ECOMMERCE STORES

ZAXY USA & CARTAGO SANDALS websites

Goal of the project:

Create ecommerce websites for Zaxy and Cartago shoes using the Vtex Cloud Commerce platform.

Technologies used:

 Front-End: HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, JavaScript, jQuery, Vtex
 Back-end: Ruby on Rails, PostgreSQL
 APIs: Vtex, CorpSystem Sales Tax SaaS (STS)
 Deployment: Heroku
 Gems: Httparty, Clockwork, Slack-notifier, Figaro

Development of the project:

These were my first ecommerce websites and my work led the company to be awarded the US Revelation Partner of 2017 by Vtex. The projects challenged me to learn a whole new platform in a limited amount of time. I also had to make sure that the integration with other systems worked seamlessly.

What I learned:

Apart from learning to use a comprehensive ecommerce platform, I acquired vast knowledge about logistics, SEO and key concepts of selling online. On the technical side, I had the opportunity to use Httparty and the Net::FTP class for the first time.
